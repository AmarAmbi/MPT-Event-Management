Karnataka offers a variety of venues, including beaches, backwaters resorts, luxury hotels, palaces, heritage buildings, traditional wedding halls, convention centers, auditoriums, Kalyana mandapams, banquet halls, and even plantations and farmhouses.

What is the Best Time for a events in karnataka?

The ideal time is generally considered to be between November and February up to March when the weather is pleasant and dry.

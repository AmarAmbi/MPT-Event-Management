Authentic Karnataka Rituals

Our team specializes in curating weddings that reflect the true essence of Karnataka. We incorporate traditional rituals, attire, music, and cuisine that honor the heritage of the region. Every element—from the mandap to the entrance arch—is designed to bring forth the warmth of age-old customs. Whether it’s the serene Nadaswaram music or the sacred rituals conducted by experienced purohits, every moment echoes authenticity.

Families and guests are treated to an immersive cultural experience. From Haldi to Muhurtham, our planners ensure that nothing is missed. All ceremonial items are arranged in accordance with community traditions. We also coordinate priest services, Sanskrit mantra booklets, and customary welcome kits for guests.

With over 15 years of experience handling various regional wedding types—Brahmin, Lingayat, Vokkaliga, Kodava, and more—we bring specialized knowledge to the table. We help you plan a flawless day from garlands to goodbyes.

Exquisite Decor

Our expert design team transforms any venue—be it palace, beach, or resort—into a dreamy tableau tailored to your vision. We believe that decor sets the tone for the entire wedding, and we ensure that every element reflects your personality, preferences, and cultural significance.

From elegant floral installations to intricate stage backdrops, we craft every detail with care. We work with a curated selection of florists, lighting artists, and artisans to bring your wedding theme to life. Whether you desire a royal Mysore-style wedding, a minimal mandap by the sea, or a colorful garden celebration, our team makes it happen flawlessly.

Traditional elements like banana trunks, mango leaves, brass urulis with floating flowers, and rangoli are blended beautifully with modern aesthetics. We also use drapes, lanterns, and thematic props that align with your chosen color palette.

Each wedding decor project begins with a visual moodboard created after understanding your preferences through meetings and consultations.

Complete Wedding Coordination

At MPT Events, we offer end-to-end wedding coordination that ensures every detail is meticulously planned and flawlessly executed. From helping you select the ideal venue—be it a royal palace in Mysore, a serene beachside resort in Gokarna, or an elegant banquet in Bengaluru—to managing a curated list of trusted vendors, we take care of it all. Our team handles everything including décor installation, sound and lighting setup, guest transportation, welcome arrangements, and accommodation bookings with precision. We coordinate all pre-wedding events like Haldi, Mehndi, and Sangeet, ensuring they flow seamlessly into the main wedding ceremony and reception. Logistics such as guest arrival tracking, VIP handling, vendor check-ins, and equipment setup are managed through detailed timelines. We liaise with photographers and videographers to ensure every important moment—from the bride’s entry to the mangalsutra ceremony—is beautifully captured.

FAQ

How far in advance should we book?

We recommend booking at least 6–12 months ahead, especially for destination or weekend weddings.

What services are included in your packages?

Our packages typically include venue setup, catering, decoration, entertainment, photography, and event coordination.

Can we customize our event theme?

Absolutely! We work closely with you to design a personalized theme that matches your vision and preferences.

Yes, our experienced coordinators are present on-site to manage the event smoothly from start to finish.

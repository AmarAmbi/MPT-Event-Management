Multi-Cuisine Catering

Whether it's a wedding, corporate event, or a birthday bash, our chefs deliver a rich culinary experience — from traditional Karnataka meals to global buffet spreads. We customize every menu to suit your theme and guests.

Our team handles everything — food styling, live counters, hygiene, and on-site service — to give you a worry-free event.

We collaborate with clients to build menus around cultural preferences, seasonal ingredients, and event style — whether it’s South Indian thali, North Indian banquets, Indo-Chinese fusion, or Mediterranean platters.

Our menu tastings before the event allow you to finalize every detail with confidence, ensuring your guests are delighted with every bite.

Live Food Stations

We offer interactive live counters for dosa, chaat, grills, Chinese stir-fry, and more. Guests love the freshness and fun of live cooking — it's an experience as much as it is a meal.

Every station is manned by professionals, with hygiene and taste being our top priority.

These stations not only elevate the food experience but also engage your guests. Watching their favorite dishes made hot and fresh right in front of them creates both excitement and personal touch.

From pasta tossed on the spot to sizzling kebabs, our live counters cater to all age groups and taste preferences, adding energy to your celebration.

Desserts & Beverages

From regional sweets like payasam to continental treats like tiramisu, our dessert counters are a crowd favorite. We also set up stylish beverage bars with mocktails, juices, and traditional drinks.

Add a sweet touch to your event that guests won’t forget.

We work with top dessert artisans to craft customized sweet tables, chocolate fountains, and fusion dessert jars that match your event’s theme and colors.

For beverages, our team curates signature mocktails, filter coffee bars, and regional favorites like buttermilk and jal jeera — ensuring something refreshing for everyone.

FAQ

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and Jain meal options. We customize as per your guest needs.

Do you provide food tasting before finalizing the menu?

Absolutely! We offer pre-event food tasting to ensure you're 100% satisfied with every dish.

What cuisines do you offer?

We offer Indian, Continental, Chinese, Italian, South Indian, North Indian, and fusion cuisines tailored to your preferences.

Is live cooking or food counters available?

Yes, we provide live counters like chaat, pasta, grill, dosa, and dessert stations to enhance the guest experience.

How is food hygiene and quality maintained?

We follow strict hygiene protocols, use fresh ingredients, and ensure all kitchen and serving staff follow FSSAI standards.
